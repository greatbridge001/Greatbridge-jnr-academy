Discipline & Conduct

School Rules

Our school rules are founded on respect, responsibility, and community. Every learner, parent, and staff member is expected to uphold these standards.

Attendance & Punctuality

  • School gates open at 6:30 AM. All learners must be in class by 7:30 AM.
  • Attendance is compulsory every school day. Absence requires a written explanation from a parent or guardian.
  • Persistent lateness of more than 3 times per term will result in a formal parental meeting.
  • Learners who miss more than 20% of a term's lessons may be required to repeat the term.

Uniform & Appearance

  • Full school uniform must be worn every day. Sports uniform is worn only on designated PE days.
  • Uniform must be clean, ironed, and worn correctly at all times including travel to and from school.
  • Hair must be neat and natural. Dyeing, perming, or extreme styles are not permitted.
  • Jewellery, makeup, and nail polish are not allowed on school premises.

Electronics & Devices

  • Mobile phones are not permitted for day scholars during school hours.
  • Boarding students may keep phones but must surrender them by 9:00 PM nightly.
  • Unauthorised devices found during school hours will be confiscated and returned only to a parent.
  • Use of school computers for non-academic purposes is prohibited.

Academic Conduct

  • All assignments and homework must be submitted on the given deadline.
  • Plagiarism and academic dishonesty in any form will result in a zero mark and disciplinary action.
  • Learners must respect classroom materials, textbooks, and school property.
  • Examinations must be sat under strict exam conditions. Any form of cheating leads to immediate disqualification.

Behaviour & Respect

  • All learners must treat fellow students, teachers, and staff with courtesy and respect at all times.
  • Bullying, verbal abuse, or physical aggression in any form is a serious offence and will not be tolerated.
  • Profane language on school premises is strictly prohibited.
  • Learners must greet teachers and visitors appropriately and maintain a polite tone at all times.

Boarding Rules

  • Lights out is at 10:00 PM. All students must be in their dorms by 9:30 PM.
  • Visiting hours are Saturdays from 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM only. Visitors must sign in at the gate.
  • Leaving campus without written permission from the school and parent is a serious offence.
  • Each student is responsible for the cleanliness and order of their personal space and dormitory area.
